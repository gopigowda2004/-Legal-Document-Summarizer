SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

Effective Date: April 1, 2025

PROVIDER: CloudWare Hosting Co., 200 Skyline Blvd, Seattle, WA 98109

CUSTOMER: GreenFields Agri LLP, 15 Meadow Lane, Des Moines, IA 50309

1. SERVICE AVAILABILITY
Provider will maintain 99.9% monthly uptime, excluding scheduled maintenance not to exceed four (4) hours per month.

2. RESPONSE AND RESOLUTION TIMES
- Critical (P1): Response within 1 hour; Resolution within 4 hours
- High (P2): Response within 4 hours; Resolution within 1 business day
- Medium (P3): Response within 1 business day; Resolution within 3 business days
- Low (P4): Response within 2 business days; Resolution within 5 business days

3. MAINTENANCE WINDOWS
Provider will notify Customer at least 48 hours prior to planned maintenance.

4. CREDITS
If monthly uptime falls below the SLA, Customer is eligible for service credits equal to 10% of the monthly fee for each 0.1% below 99.9%, capped at 50% of the monthly fee.

5. SUPPORT
Support is available 24/7 via email and ticketing system. Phone support during business hours 9am–6pm PT.

6. SECURITY
Provider will maintain industry-standard security controls, including encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access, and regular vulnerability scanning.

7. TERM AND TERMINATION
This SLA remains effective for the duration of the Subscription Agreement. Either party may terminate upon thirty (30) days’ written notice for material breach.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Washington State.
